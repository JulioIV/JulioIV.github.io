What you should know:

Born: December 11, 1989 - Bronx, New York

I am an artist raised in the slums of New York and currently living in South Florida. I call
myself an artist because claiming what your specialty is categorizes your skill set at
everything else. My skills include graphic design, web design, written work such as:
poetry, short stories, song writing, & etc. & I love to dabble with canvas
& camera art as well. If there is anything I ever leanered from stereotypes,
it's that they will always be there but its up to you to break them thus, I
am an artist. Nothing less.

I was born in the South Bronx to an immigrant Dominican father and a Cuban-Japanese mother.
My mother loved to travel whereever she worked and me being a huge "Momma's boy",
my bags were always packed. From New York, to Chicago, to South Beach, I learned
as much as could whereever we lived increasing my contact list, knowledge, &
of course my passion for the arts.

I enlisted in the United States military straight out of highschool and 8years
years later, I am still a proud enlisted member of The Florida Army
National Gaurd. I am currently studying web design at the
Miami International University of Art & Design. Let's spread art!
